OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH & SAFETY POLICY

> OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH & SAFETY POLICY

The management of SAUDI TAM is dedicated to preventing any unintentional loss of its assets, including personnel and tangible property. Our goal is to operate in the safest manner possible while always working toward a workplace free of injuries.

M anagement shall provide and maintain a safe and healthy work environment in compliance with industry standards and legal requirements to safeguard individuals and property. It shall also endeavor to eradicate any foreseeable hazards that may lead to property damage, accidents, personal injury, or illness.

We acknowledge that there is shared accountability for health and safety. To reduce accidents both on our job sites and within our facilities, every employee will share equal responsibility. All staff members will get explicit instructions and communication regarding safe work practices and job procedures.

By combining proactive staff involvement with sound management practices, accidental losses can be reduced. Within SAUDI TAM, all managers, supervisors, staff members, and subcontractors bear direct responsibility for maintaining safety..

In terms of facility and equipment planning, construction, operation, and maintenance, all SAUDI TAM activities will adhere to the company’s safety regulations. Every employee shall carry out their duties correctly, following established protocols and safe work practices.
