QUALITY POLICY

> QUALITY POLICY

SAUDI TAM. is adamant that attaining “Building A Stronger Future” requires a commitment to quality. In order to meet or beyond customers’ expectations, the company is committed to providing high-quality and value-added Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) Services. This dedication is streamlined by the Quality Management System.

The Management of the Company is dedicated to:

Create, record, put into practice, and uphold a quality management system; continuously enhance its efficacy by adhering to or surpassing the most recent ISO 9001 International Standard, relevant laws, and the needs of clients and interested parties.

Employees should receive expert instruction to develop both their technical and soft abilities.

Keep an eye on employee, supplier, and customer satisfaction.

Through teamwork and process measurement, make ongoing changes to desired aims and quality objectives to guarantee their applicability and achievability

Make use of the most recent technologies and technical breakthroughs.

We work hard to make sure that our business practices, which prioritize quality and safety, establish a standard with all relevant parties.

Periodically, the quality policy is examined to ensure that it remains appropriate for the organization.
